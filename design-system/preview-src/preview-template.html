06 · Rules

Do & Don't

Digest from DESIGN.md — the rules that keep every screen unmistakably MIDU.

✓ Do

  • Route every primary action through one gradient pill.
  • Gradient always flows indigo → magenta (only the footer may reverse it, as a "closing bracket").
  • Alternate white and lavender-tint sections so long pages breathe.
  • Ink text on yellow surfaces — white on yellow fails contrast at every size.
  • Nutrient bubbles are the default decoration before inventing any new shape.
  • Every growth claim gets a caption source line.

✗ Don't

  • No gradient on body text, icons, borders, or a second CTA.
  • No black borders on UI — comic outlines belong to MIGI and illustration.
  • Never swap Sun (#EFCA3D) with MIGI Yellow (#FFDA69).
  • No new hues beyond the defined system.
  • No square corners, no rectangular buttons.
  • No tiny MIGI (favicons) — use the lightbulb dot or the logo "m".
